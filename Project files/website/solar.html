ENERGY

Where from here?

Solar is just the beginning. We're on a path toward a diversified and greener energy landscape. Wind energy will continue to grow, with larger and more efficient turbines. Offshore wind farms, in particular, hold great potential. Hydropower will become more sustainable through innovations like small-scale hydroelectric generators and fish-friendly turbines. Modernizing and retrofitting existing dams will also enhance their efficiency. Advances in enhanced geothermal systems (EGS) will expand the reach of geothermal energy. Sustainable biomass sources, such as algae and crop residues, will play a role in bioenergy production. Tidal and wave energy technologies will mature, harnessing the power of ocean movements. Green hydrogen produced using renewable energy sources will gain prominence as an energy carrier and storage medium.
